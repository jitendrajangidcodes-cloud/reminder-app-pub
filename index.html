Reminder

A reminder app for Android that behaves like an alarm clock — fires exact-time alarms that ring and vibrate even from a locked screen.

What it does

  • Schedule one-time and repeating reminders (daily, weekly, monthly, custom intervals).
  • Three alert intensities — from a quiet notification to a full-screen alarm that wakes the device.
  • Optional backup & sync of your reminders to your own Google Drive and a Google Sheet.

Privacy

The app stores reminders locally on your device. Cloud backup is optional and writes only to your own Google account.
