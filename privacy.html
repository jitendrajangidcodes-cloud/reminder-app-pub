Privacy Policy

App: Reminder (Android package com.pnsjy.reminder_app)
Last updated: 30 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data the Reminder app ("the app", "we") handles, how it is used, and your choices. The app is a personal reminder/alarm tool. We aim to collect as little data as possible.

1. Data stored on your device

Your reminders — titles, notes, categories, schedules, ringtone choices, attached images or links, and a history log of alarm actions — are stored locally in a private database on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable Cloud Backup & Sync (see below).

2. Google account & cloud backup (optional)

The app offers an optional feature to back up and sync your reminders to your own Google account. If — and only if — you choose to sign in with Google and enable this feature, the app requests these Google permissions:

  • Google Drive — app data folder (drive.appdata): a hidden, app-specific folder in your own Google Drive used to store a backup copy of your reminders. This folder is not visible alongside your normal Drive files and is used solely by this app.
  • Google Sheets (spreadsheets): to create and update a single human-readable spreadsheet in your own Google account that mirrors your reminders, so you can read your backup outside the app.

We use these permissions only to read and write your own reminder data for backup and sync. We do not access any other files, sheets, or Drive content. Your basic Google profile (name, email, profile picture) is used only to show you are signed in.

3. How your data is used

  • To schedule and fire your reminders/alarms on your device.
  • To back up and restore your reminders to and from your own Google Drive/Sheets when you enable sync.

We do not sell your data, share it with third parties, use it for advertising, or use it to train any machine-learning models.

4. Where your data lives

Your reminder data is stored (a) locally on your device and (b) if you enable sync, in your own Google Drive and Google Sheets. We do not operate our own servers and do not keep a copy of your data on any backend we control. Google's handling of the data stored in your account is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy.

5. Google API Services — Limited Use disclosure

The app's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Permissions on your device

The app requests Android permissions needed to act as an alarm clock — exact alarms, notifications, full-screen alarm display, vibration, and (optionally) access to media you choose to attach. These are used only to deliver your reminders.

7. Data deletion

You can delete reminders inside the app at any time. To remove cloud backups, you can sign out and delete the app's data folder and backup spreadsheet from your Google account, or uninstall the app. Uninstalling removes all locally stored data.

8. Children

The app is a general-purpose utility and is not directed at children under 13.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
